Chocolate Crinkle Cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup unsweeted cocoa powder
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter melted and slightly cooled
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup light brown sugar packed
  • 4 large eggs at room temperature
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar

Instructions

  1. In large mixing bowl, sift flour and cocoa powder together, then whisk in the baking powder and salt. Set aside.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together melted butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until well combined. Mix in the eggs and vanilla until fully combined.
  3. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and mix until just combined.
  4. Cover tightly and cool in fridge for at least 2 hours.
  5. Preheat oven to 350F. Line large baking sheets with parchment paper. Add powdered sugar to small bowl, set aside.
  6. Remove cookie dough from the fridge. Using a tablespoon, scoop cookie dough and roll into a ball. Roll ball in the powdered sugar, and place balls onto the baking sheet.
  7. Bake for 11-13 mins or until the tops of the cookies are set. Remove from the oven and allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheets for 5 - 10 mins, then carefully transfer cookies to wire cooling rack to finish cooling.
