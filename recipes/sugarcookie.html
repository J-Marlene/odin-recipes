Soft Sugar Cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup salted butter
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar for rolling cookies

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375F. Line 2 baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, baking powder and salt. Set aside.
  3. Place butter and sugar in large mixing bowl. Mix on medium-high speed until light in color and fluffy, about 3 mins.
  4. Add egg and vanilla and mix on low for about 30 seconds or until fully incorporated.
  5. Slowly add in dry ingredients, be careful not to over mix.
  6. Scoop the dough into large balls, roll the ball in the sugar.
  7. Place balls on your lined baking sheets. Bake for 12-13 mins. Cookies are done when the edges are a light golden brown and there is some crackling along the top. Do not over bake as the cookies will not be as chewy.
  8. Cool for 5 mins on the baking sheet. Transfer cookies carefully to the cooling rack to finish cooling. Cookies stay fresh for up to one week. (If they last that long)
