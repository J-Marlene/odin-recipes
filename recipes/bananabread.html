Banana Bread

Image of baked banana bread

This is one of my favorite recipes to use up bananas that have gotten brown on the counter, that no one eats anyways lol

Ingredients

  • 3 bananas
  • Milk
  • Flour
  • melted butter

Directions

  1. Mash Bananas
  2. Mix with melted butter and milk
  3. Mix in Flour
